About us

CPHR is a Danish-owned employment agency specializing in Human Resources, providing our clients with the competent and experienced staff for short-term or long-time employment.

CPHR works side-by-side with a Polish recruitment agency, highly qualified in finding employees with the suitable expertise for any and every task in construction, industrial, agricultural and any manual labour niches.

All of our employees are living temporarily in Denmark, with all living expenses covered by us, meaning that the only expense of our customers is the salary of the employee. Termination of employees happen day-to-day and without prior notice. CPHR can readily replace a worker or find additional workers if needed.
